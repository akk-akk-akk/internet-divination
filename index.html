Magic in the Machine



This project investigates the intersection of:

  • machine / magic
  • technology / divination
  • rational / irrational
  • data / intuition



Is magic really that different from technology? We used to seek meaning and guidance from stars, gods,nature. In this post-internet age, where do our instincts rooted in the spiritual go? Sometimes, its flipping a coin, opening an astrology app, or even asking ChatGPT a question.

In a world of black box algorithms, operations that predict outcomes are no more than a divine oracles subject to biases, misinformation or misdirection disguised as objective truth. Regardless, its increasing prevalence and progress point towards a future where AI models are believed to be a primary source for meaning-making and decision-making.

Told through the perspective of a burnt-out Microsoft Agent (Merlin the Setup Wizard), Magic in the Machine experiments with digital oracles for a poetic investigation of meaning-making, faith, and technoculture.
