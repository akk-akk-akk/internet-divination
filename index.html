The Internet is a Space for Divination

Despite its contentious nature in a world dominated by science and rationality, divination has been proved to be ubiquitous across various cultures. Having established the pervasiveness of divination in our present society also comes with the recognition that it is not a practice immune to exploitation. Particularly in the post-internet age, increasingly new technologies serve as fresh opportunities for users to find meaning from.

This research therefore positions divination as a valuable source of cultural investigation, its influences and its pitfalls. Through the lens of semiotic analysis and speculative design, we then ask the question — how can divination be used as a driving force for social change?
